Project overview

Little Brook Learning Center needed a clearer digital foundation that could support parent communication, enrollment conversion, and long-term maintainability.

The implementation was split across dedicated frontend, backend, and UI/UX repositories to keep collaboration clean and enable faster iteration without coupling design and application logic.
